Auto Record Moments

An Android app that continuously records audio to a temporary buffer, ensuring you never miss a moment.

Account Deletion Instructions

To permanently delete your Auto Record Moments account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Auto Record Moments app on your device.
  2. Navigate to the Settings screen (tap the gear icon in the top bar).
  3. If you are signed in, you will see a "Delete Account" section at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Tap the "DELETE MY ACCOUNT" button.
  5. Follow the on-screen confirmation prompts to complete the deletion.

This process will permanently remove your account and all synced settings from our servers. This action cannot be undone.
